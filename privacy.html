Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

CrossBot℠ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle data:

  • Data Collection: CrossBot℠ does not collect, log, store, or transmit any user data, messages, server histories, or personal information.
  • Message Processing: Messages are processed entirely in real-time within memory via Bot Designer for Discord to check for text triggers and are immediately discarded.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Because no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.
